What is Personal Loan EMI?

Personal Loan EMI is the fixed monthly amount paid by a borrower to repay a personal loan. It includes both principal repayment and interest charged by the lender.

Personal Loan EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1 + R)N / [(1 + R)N – 1]

  • P = Personal Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Number of Monthly Installments

Example of Personal Loan EMI Calculation

If you take a personal loan of ₹3,00,000 at an interest rate of 14% per annum for 4 years, your monthly EMI will be approximately ₹8,172.

Factors Affecting Personal Loan EMI

  • Loan amount
  • Interest rate
  • Loan tenure
  • Credit score

FAQs

Is personal loan EMI fixed?

Yes, for fixed-rate personal loans, EMI remains the same throughout the tenure.

Can personal loan be prepaid?

Yes, most banks and NBFCs allow prepayment, which reduces total interest payable.
