What is Bike Loan EMI?

Bike Loan EMI is the fixed monthly amount paid to repay a two-wheeler loan. It includes both interest and principal repayment.

Bike Loan EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1 + R)N / [(1 + R)N – 1]

  • P = Bike Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Number of Monthly Installments

Example of Bike Loan EMI Calculation

If you take a bike loan of ₹1,00,000 at an interest rate of 12% per annum for 3 years, your monthly EMI will be approximately ₹3,321.

Factors Affecting Bike Loan EMI

  • Bike price
  • Interest rate
  • Loan tenure
  • Down payment

FAQs

Is bike loan EMI fixed?

Yes, for fixed-rate bike loans, EMI remains the same throughout the tenure.

Can bike loan be prepaid?

Yes, most banks allow prepayment, which reduces interest payable.
